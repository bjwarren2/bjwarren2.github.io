Park Ranger Intern

  • July 2020 - November 2020
  • NC DNCR – William B. Umstead State Park
  • Internship (425 contact hours)

As a Park Ranger Intern at William B. Umstead State Park, I collaborated closely with park rangers to ensure efficient park operations, including patrol and law enforcement, resource protection and management, and visitor safety. I conducted regular patrols of the park and provided law enforcement services such as traffic control and enforcement of park regulations. Additionally, I conducted investigations related to criminal activity within the park to maintain a safe and secure environment for park visitors.

   

From this experience, I acquired a variety of valuable skills, including: 

Law enforcement: As a Park Ranger Intern, I gained hands-on experience in law enforcement, including traffic control, park regulation enforcement, and conducting criminal investigations.

Collaboration: I worked closely with park rangers and other team members to ensure efficient park operations and maintain visitor safety.

Resource management: I gained knowledge and experience in managing park resources, including protecting the environment and maintaining park infrastructure.

Problem-solving: As a Park Ranger Intern, I faced various challenges and obstacles and developed effective problem-solving skills to overcome them.
